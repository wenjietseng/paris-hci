SIGCHI Paris

Human-Computer Interaction and Information Visualization

The goal of the Parisian SIGCHI chapter is to provide a means for all HCI researchers and practitioners of
the Parisian region to come together, discuss topics in our field and promote collaboration. We hope to become
a default forum where we can meet for talks, publicize and organize hci related events in Paris, etc.
If you'd like to join us or learn more about SIGCHI Paris.
